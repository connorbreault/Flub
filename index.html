• Based out of Sacramento, CA, Flub is comprised of former and current members of Rivers of Nihil, Alterbeast, Vale of Pnath, Psychosomatic and Becomes Astral. Influenced by a world of different sounds stretching from Tech Death, Metalcore, Progressive Metal and Deathcore; to sounds of Latin, Neo-Classical, and Jazz. Flub aims to bring the unexpected, in a relentless face-shattering color-bursting demented fantasy.

    1. Vocals - Michael Alvarez
    2. Guitar / Songwriter / Programming - Eloy Montes
    3. Drums - Jared Klein
    4. Guitar - Viktor Hansen
    5. Bass - Liam Frith
